Sabores da Matola — Site Plan

Website Planning

Site Name

Sabores da Matola

The name “Sabores da Matola” represents a restaurant located in city of Matola - Maputo, Mozambique, specializing in traditional Mozambican cuisine. I shose the name to convey the local essence and authentic flavors of the region.

Domain: saboresdamatola.co.mz

Site Purpose

The website serves as an informative and interactive platform for the restaurant Sabores da Matola. It presents the menu of traditional dishes, provides details about the restaurant, and allows customers to make reservations or add their favorite dishes to a personalized list. The main goal is to promote Mozambican culinary culture and strengthen the restaurant’s connection with clients.

Scenarios

  • What are the most popular dishes served at Sabores da Matola?
  • How can I make a table reservation directly through the website?
  • Where can I find the restaurant’s address and contact number?

Color Scheme

  • Orange (#D35400): used for the header and main buttons.
  • Green (#27AE60): used for highlight elements and icons.
  • Dark Blue (#2C3E50): used for titles and footer background.
  • Light Gray (#ECF0F1): used as the main background color.

Typography

  • Poppins — used for headings and titles.
  • Roboto — used for body text.
